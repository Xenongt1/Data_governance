ANALYSIS:
------------------------------------------------------------
  - Data structure preserved: 10 rows, 10 columns
  - PII masked: first_name, last_name, email, phone, address, date_of_birth
  - Business data intact: customer_id, income, account_status, created_date

  What analytics teams CAN still do with masked data:
    - Analyse income distributions across account statuses
    - Count active vs inactive vs suspended customers
    - Track account creation trends over time
    - Segment customers by year of birth (year preserved in DOB)

  What analytics teams CANNOT do (by design):
    - Contact customers directly (emails/phones masked)
    - Identify specific individuals (names masked)
    - Locate customers physically (addresses masked)

  Compliance: This masked dataset is safe to share under GDPR/CCPA
  because no individual can be re-identified from the masked fields.
